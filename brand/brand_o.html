Prism Map Description

The Prism Map is a 1000 x 1000 kilometer world the Prism series takes place in. The world itself is not round, and after going out a certain distance, the world slowly transitions into the void.

The name of the world primarly revolves around the idea that its environments are varied and seperated similar to a prism scattering colors.

Environments range from lush jungles, vertical mountains, barren deserts, frozen tundras, and molten canyons.
